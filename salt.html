Himalayan Pink Rock Salt

Himalayan pink salt is rock salt (halite) mined naturally without any chemicals. The salt often has a pinkish tint due to mineral impurities. It is primarily used as a food additive as table salt, but is also used as a material for cooking and food presentation, decorative lamps, and spa treatments.
